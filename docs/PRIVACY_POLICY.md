# Privacy Policy - WinClean

**Last updated: February 28, 2026**

## Overview

WinClean is a local disk cleanup utility for Windows. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how WinClean handles your data.

## Data Collection

**WinClean does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.**

Specifically, WinClean:
- Does **not** collect any personal information
- Does **not** track usage behavior or analytics
- Does **not** connect to the internet
- Does **not** send any data to external servers
- Does **not** use cookies or any tracking technologies
- Does **not** contain advertisements

## Local Data

WinClean stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- **User preferences** (theme, language, settings) stored in `%AppData%\WinClean\settings.json`
- **Application logs** stored in the application directory for troubleshooting purposes

This data never leaves your device and can be deleted at any time by the user.

## File Operations

WinClean scans and manages files on your local disk as directed by you. All file operations (scanning, deletion) are performed locally and only when explicitly initiated by the user. Deleted files are sent to the Recycle Bin by default.

## Third-Party Services

WinClean does not integrate with or use any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect data.

## Children's Privacy

WinClean does not collect any data from any users, including children under 13.
